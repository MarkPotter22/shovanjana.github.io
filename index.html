About Me

I am a Data Analyst at Bintix, Hyderabad, where I help drive sustainable solutions through advanced analytics and innovative data science. With a background in Sustainable Engineering (M.Tech, IIT Hyderabad) and experience in both data analysis and operations, I thrive on tackling real-world challenges that make a difference—both for business and the planet. From modeling hydrogen yield prediction, solar energy prediction using machine learning to optimizing energy procurement for lower costs and carbon emissions, my projects have fused technical expertise with a commitment to impactful change.

At Bintix, I transform complex waste management data into actionable strategies, empowering teams to achieve operational excellence and measurable sustainability goals.

My skill set combines proficiency in Python, SQL, machine learning, deep learning, and data visualization with strong teamwork, leadership, and organizational skills. Driven by curiosity and a desire to contribute positively, I bring a collaborative approach—whether developing predictive dashboards, helping fellow students as a teaching assistant, or coordinating institutional placements.

Outside work, I’m an avid reader with a love for both Bengali and English literature, an active participant in community service, and a keen sports enthusiast.

If you’re looking for a data expert who bridges analytics, science and environmental good, let’s connect and make an impact together.
